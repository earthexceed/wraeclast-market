Wraeclast Market — PoE & PoE2 Trade Tool

## Summary (from package, ≤132 chars)

PoE & PoE2 trade tools: stat filters, quality simulator, PoB export, poe.ninja prices, mod highlighting, bookmarks & more.

## Description (≤16,000 chars)

Wraeclast Market enhances the official Path of Exile and Path of Exile 2 trade sites with quality-of-life tools that make searching, pricing and organizing items faster.

FEATURES

• Apply stat filters from results — inline min/max boxes next to every mod. Tick the mods you care about, hit Apply, and your search re-runs in place (no page reload) — fast and light on the trade site's rate limit. Reads each mod's exact stat id, so local/global and fractured/desecrated/crafted variants are matched correctly, and mods with no number (e.g. "Cannot be Ignited") are filterable by presence. Untick a mod to drop its filter; a hide/show eye tucks the controls away when they cover a long mod.

• Quality simulator (rings & amulets) — pick a quality category and a quality %, and the affected mods rescale live in green so you can see the item at any quality. On PoE 2 it uses the Breach-catalyst categories with presets sized to the real caps (amulet up to 40%, ring up to 60% via a Breach Ring + Essence of the Breach); on PoE 1 it uses the Catalyst groups (Attack, Life and Mana, Resistance, …) with the 20% cap.

• Quality projection (weapons & armour) — see weapon Physical Damage and armour defences projected to 20% quality for at-a-glance comparison.

• Copy for Path of Building — one click copies a listing in PoB import format; paste straight into Path of Building.

• Copy for Craft of Exile — one click copies a listing in Craft of Exile's import format; paste into craftofexile.com (PoE2) to load the item's base with its mods matched, ready to plan crafts.

• Equivalent pricing — see each listing's price converted via poe.ninja's currency-exchange rates, right on the results (PoE 1 prices shown in Divine and Chaos).

• Highlight searched mods — instantly spot the mods you searched for on each result.

• Regroup similar results — collapse duplicate listings so the page is easier to scan.

• Bookmarks — save your searches into folders, complete with class/ascendancy and currency icons (including PoE2 patch 0.5 ascendancies).

• Pin items — keep an item card floating for quick reference.

Works on both Path of Exile and Path of Exile 2 trade sites.

Wraeclast Market is a fan-made, open-source fork of Better Trading. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Grinding Gear Games. All data stays on your machine — the extension only talks to pathofexile.com and poe.ninja.
